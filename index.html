Zhirui Chen

I am currently a fourth-year PhD student at the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering and Management, National University of Singapore (NUS), where I am fortunately advised by Prof. Vincent Y.F. Tan and Prof. Yeow Meng Chee. Previously, I received the B.Eng. degree from the School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University, China, in 2017.

My research interests focus on online learning, reinforcement learning and differential privacy. Additionally, I harbor a keen interest in the field of deep learning.
